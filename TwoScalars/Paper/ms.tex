If the prior is $\pi(\mathbf{x})$, then a family of canonical distributions
is given by
\begin{eqnarray}
p(\mathbf{x} | \lambda_1, ..., \lambda_n) = 
\frac{\pi(\mathbf{x})\exp(-\sum_{i=1}^n \lambda_i f_i(\mathbf{x}))}
{\mathcal{Z}(\lambda_1, ..., \lambda_n)}
\end{eqnarray}
